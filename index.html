The format of each stave is:

[Time signature] [note/octave:duration] x N

The time signature is: 

[number of beat]/[beat value]

Example: 6/4 e/4:8 e/4:8 a/4:q a/4:q b/4:q c/5:h

The number of notes combined with the duration of each should be coherent with the time signature

duration can be a number or letter which correspond to the fraction of a beat: [16 8 4 2 1] or [q h w]

You can edit the title by clicking on it, and save your music sheet (it save in the local storage of your browser)
